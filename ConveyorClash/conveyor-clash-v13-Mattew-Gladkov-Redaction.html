HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Destroy enemy Core before they destroy yours. Cores are only vulnerable when the defending player is dead (respawning)!

Energy System

Energy is your main resource. Start with 55, max 280. There are Energy Nodes on the map. Build Conveyors from your Core to nodes to capture them. Each captured node = +2.0 energy/sec. No nodes = only +1.0/sec.

Node Capture Rules

Each player has 2 nodes on their half with owner priority — the field owner always captures them first. The central node is contested: first player to connect a conveyor to it captures it. If both players connect, the node becomes contested (neutral) until one side is cut off. Opponents can only drain energy from your nodes if they are NOT connected to your conveyor network.

Buildings

Conveyor [1] — 3E
Energy pipes. Build chains from your Core toward nodes. Connected conveyors glow bright with arrows. Disconnected ones show red x. You walk through them freely. Conveyors are invulnerable while you are alive! 0.8s placement cooldown (shared with Walls). Wall [2] — 6E
Light barrier (21 HP). Blocks enemy bullets and movement. You can walk through YOUR OWN walls! Destroyed by 1 melee hit, 3 ranged shots, or 1 mob hit. Walls do NOT benefit from conveyor armor. 0.8s placement cooldown (shared with Conveyors). Turret [3] — 15E (4s cooldown)
Auto-fires at enemies (320px range). Each shot costs 1E from your pool. 100 HP. Deals 6 dmg per shot. Max 3 turrets. 8s rebuild cooldown when destroyed. Destroyed by 2 melee hits or 6 ranged shots! Spawner [4] — 20E (6s cooldown)
Spawns kamikaze mobs every 5s (max 4 per spawner). Mobs rush toward enemies, deal damage on contact, then die. 50 HP each. Deals 10 dmg to players, 15 to buildings, 35 to walls, 12 to cores. 10s rebuild cooldown when destroyed.

Core Vulnerability

A Core can only take damage when its defending player is dead and respawning (4s). This creates strategic windows — eliminate the enemy, then rush their core! Bullets deal 50% damage to cores, melee deals 70%, turret shots deal 40%. Melee is the best way to destroy cores!

Combat

Shoot [LMB] — 3E per bolt. 7 dmg to mechs, 17 to buildings.
Melee [RMB] — 30 dmg to players, 50 to buildings. 0.85s CD.
Dash [Space] — 8 impact dmg. Invincible briefly. 0.7s CD.
Shield [Q hold] — Projects a shield that blocks 1 hit (bullet or melee). After shield breaks, 1.7s cooldown before recharging. Shield only protects in a forward arc.

Overtime

After 4.5 minutes, both Cores start losing HP (5/s). After 5.5 min: 12/s. After 6.5 min: 35/s. This ensures decisive outcomes!

Tips

Capture nodes early for economy! Starting energy covers ~70% of conveyor path to nearest node. Conveyors are protected while you live. Eliminate the enemy player to expose their core! Melee deals the most core damage per hit. Buildings near connected conveyors get 30% damage reduction (except walls). Walls are light barriers. Shield blocks 1 hit then breaks — time it well! Spawner mobs are kamikaze — they hit once then die. Turrets die in 2 melee hits or 6 shots.
