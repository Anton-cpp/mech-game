HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Destroy enemy Core before they destroy yours. Cores are only vulnerable when the defending player is dead (respawning)!

Energy System

Energy is your main resource. Start with 50, max 350. There are Energy Nodes on the map. Build Conveyors from your Core to nodes to capture them. Each captured node = +3.5 energy/sec. No nodes = only +0.8/sec.

Node Capture Rules

Each player has 2 nodes on their half with owner priority — the field owner always captures them first. The central node is contested: first player to connect a conveyor to it captures it. If both players connect, the node becomes contested (neutral) until one side is cut off. Opponents can only drain energy from your nodes if they are NOT connected to your conveyor network.

Buildings

Conveyor [1] — 3E
Energy pipes. Build chains from your Core toward nodes. Connected conveyors glow bright with arrows. Disconnected ones show red x. You walk through them freely. NEW v13.5: Fresh conveyors are vulnerable for 4s after placement (yellow pulse + countdown) — enemies can destroy them in this window! After the window expires, they become invulnerable while you are alive. Wall [2] — 5E
Barrier (120 HP). Blocks enemy bullets and movement. You can walk through YOUR OWN walls! Turret [3] — 16E (5s cooldown)
Auto-fires at enemies (320px range). Each shot costs 1E from your pool. 70 HP. Spawner [4] — 20E (8s cooldown)
Spawns NPC mobs every 8s (max 3 per spawner). Mobs march toward the enemy core. 90 HP.

Core Vulnerability

A Core can only take damage when its defending player is dead and respawning (3.5s). This creates strategic windows — eliminate the enemy, then rush their core!

Combat

Shoot [LMB] — 4E per bolt.
Melee [RMB] — 28 dmg, destroys buildings! 0.75s CD.
Dash [Space] — 18 impact dmg. Invincible briefly. 1.4s CD.
Parry [Q hold] — Reflects bullets at 1.5x dmg.

Power-Ups

Random power-ups spawn in the center of the map every 20-30 seconds! Walk over them to collect:
Speed Boost — 1.4x movement speed for 8 seconds
Shield — Absorbs 40 damage (one-time)
Rapid Fire — 2x fire rate for 6 seconds
Beware of the Lava Zone in the center — it deals 15 damage/sec! The lava shifts position every 30 seconds.

Upgrades

Strike [E] — 50/100/160E
Improves melee range and damage. Level 3 adds AoE cleave (hits multiple targets in a wide arc).

Bullet [R] — 60/130/220E
Increases bullet speed and damage. Level 3 makes bullets nearly instant — no need to lead targets!

Fortify [F] — 60/110/170E
Increases max HP (+25/50/80) and reduces all incoming damage by 5%/10%/15%.

Tips

Capture nodes early for economy! Conveyors are protected once settled (4s after build). Eliminate the enemy player to expose their core!
