Objective

Destroy the enemy Core before they destroy yours. A core is only vulnerable when all defending players on that team are dead (respawning)!

v14: Teams

2 vs 2 mode — each team has 2 players sharing one core and one energy pool. If a teammate is missing, a bot fills the slot. The core becomes vulnerable only when both team members are eliminated. Coordinate with your teammate: one builds economy, one pressures the enemy!

v14: Construction Vulnerability

Freshly placed conveyors are vulnerable for 4 seconds (yellow pulsing border, reduced HP). After that they become invulnerable while their owner team has a living player. This means opponents CAN disrupt your fresh construction — don't just run and build, watch your back! Established networks stay safe.

v14.5: Combo Meter & Critical Hits

Get kills within 10 seconds to build a combo! DOUBLE KILL, TRIPLE KILL, QUAD KILL, RAMPAGE announcements appear on screen. The combo HUD shows your streak with a decay bar. Melee hits have a 15% chance to be critical — dealing extra damage with red "CRIT!" text.

v14.4: Replay System

Every practice game is automatically recorded. Click View Replay in the menu to watch a playback with play/pause, step controls, and a Core HP timeline graph. Click on the minimap during gameplay to ping locations for your team.

v14.3: Settings & Pause

Press Esc during a game to open the settings panel (pauses the game in practice mode). Toggle minimap, screen shake, match time limit, and volume. Match History tracks your last 20 games with W/L record.

v14.2: Bot Difficulty

Choose Easy, Normal, or Hard bots. Hard bots react faster, shoot more accurately, and build more aggressively. Difficulty persists across sessions.

Energy System

Energy is your shared team resource. Start with 50, max 350. There are Energy Nodes on the map. Build Conveyors from your Core to nodes to capture them. Each captured node = +3.5 energy/sec. No nodes = only +0.8/sec.

Node Capture Rules

Each team has 2 nodes on their half with owner priority. The central node pair is contested: first team to connect a conveyor captures it. If both connect, the node becomes contested (neutral) until one side is cut off.

Buildings

Conveyor [1] — 3E, 1s cooldown
Energy pipes from your Core toward nodes. Connected conveyors glow bright with arrows. Disconnected ones show red x. Vulnerable for 4s after placement, then invulnerable while a teammate is alive.

Wall [2] — 5E, no cooldown
Barrier (120 HP). Blocks enemy bullets and movement. You walk through YOUR team's walls!

Turret [3] — 16E (5s cooldown)
Auto-fires at enemies (320px range). Each shot costs 1E. 70 HP.

Spawner [4] — 20E (8s cooldown)
Spawns NPC mobs every 8s (max 3 per spawner). Mobs march toward the enemy core. 90 HP.

Combat

Shoot [LMB] — 4E per bolt.
Melee [RMB] — 28 dmg, destroys buildings! 0.75s CD.
Dash [Space] — 18 impact dmg. Invincible briefly. 1.4s CD.
Parry [Q hold] — Reflects bullets at 1.5x dmg.

Power-Ups & Lava

Random power-ups spawn in the center every 20-30s: Speed, Shield, Rapid Fire. Beware the Lava Zone — 15 dmg/sec, shifts every 30s.

Upgrades

[8] Strike — improves melee range/damage. Lv3 adds AoE cleave.
[9] Bullet — increases bullet speed/damage. Lv3 = near-instant.
[0] Fortify — +HP and damage reduction.

Tips

In 2v2, split roles: one player captures nodes & defends, the other harasses the enemy. Cover your teammate's fresh conveyor placements. Eliminate both enemies to expose their core!
