HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Destroy enemy Core before they destroy yours. Cores are only vulnerable when the defending player is dead (respawning)!

Energy System

Energy is your main resource. Start with 50, max 350. Build Conveyors from your Core to nodes. Each captured node = +3.5 energy/sec.

Node Capture Rules

Each player has 2 nodes on their half with owner priority. The central node is contested.

Buildings

Conveyor [1] — 3E — Energy pipes. Connected conveyors glow. Disconnected show red x. Invulnerable while you live!
Wall [2] — 5E — 120 HP barrier. Walk through your own!
Turret [3] — 16E (5s CD) — Auto-fires, 70 HP, each shot costs 1E.
Spawner [4] — 20E (8s CD) — Spawns NPC mobs, max 3 per spawner.

Core Vulnerability

A Core can only take damage when its defending player is dead (3.5s respawn).

Combat

Shoot [LMB] — 4E per bolt. Melee [RMB] — 28 dmg. Dash [Space] — 18 dmg, invincible. Parry [Q hold] — Reflects bullets.

Power-Ups & Lava

Random power-ups spawn in center. Lava Zone deals 15 dmg/sec, shifts every 30s.

Upgrades

Strike [8] — 50/100/160E. Bullet [9] — 60/130/220E. Fortify [0] — 60/110/170E.

Tips

Capture nodes early! Conveyors are protected while you live. Eliminate enemy to expose their core!
