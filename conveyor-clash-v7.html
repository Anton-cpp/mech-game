HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Destroy enemy Core before they destroy yours. Cores are only vulnerable when the defending player is dead (respawning)!

Energy System

Energy is your main resource. Start with 50, max 350. There are Energy Nodes (hexagons with "E") on the map. Build Conveyors from your Core to nodes to capture them. Each captured node = +3.5 energy/sec. No nodes = only +0.8/sec.

Buildings

Conveyor [1] — 3E
Energy pipes. Build chains from your Core toward nodes. Connected conveyors glow bright with arrows. Disconnected ones show red X. You walk through them freely. Wall [2] — 5E (max 8)
Barrier (120 HP). Blocks enemy bullets and movement. You can walk through YOUR OWN walls! Max 8 walls per player. Turret [3] — 16E (5s cooldown between placements)
Auto-fires at enemies (320px range). Each shot costs 1E from your pool. 70 HP. You can walk through YOUR OWN turrets! Spawner [4] — 20E
Spawns NPC mobs every 8 seconds (max 3 active per spawner). Mobs march toward the enemy core, attacking buildings and players. 90 HP.

Core Vulnerability

A Core can only take damage when its defending player is dead and respawning (3.5s). This creates strategic windows — eliminate the enemy, then rush their core!

How Energy Network Works

1. Build Conveyors from your Core (they auto-chain).
2. Extend toward Energy Nodes on the map.
3. When conveyor touches a node -> you capture it (+3.5E/s each).
4. More energy = more shooting + turrets auto-fire!

Combat

Shoot [LMB] — 4E per bolt. Fast rate.
Melee [RMB] — 28 dmg, destroys buildings! 0.75s CD.
Dash [Space] — 18 impact dmg. Invincible briefly. 1.4s CD.
Parry [Q hold] — Reflects bullets at 1.5x dmg.

Tips

• Capture nodes early for economy!
• You can walk through your own walls and turrets.
• Spawner mobs pressure the enemy while you focus on combat.
• Eliminate the enemy player to expose their core!
• Press P to pause bot in practice mode.
